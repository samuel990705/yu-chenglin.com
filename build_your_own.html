Get Started

To get started in building your PC, you must first plan out everything. It would be a pain if you start building you PC then realize some of your parts are not compatible. Picking your parts is probably one of the most crucial step of building your PC.

Websites I Used

The two main websites I used are PC Part Picker and YouTube. PC Part Picker is a great site that helps you plan out your parts in your PC. It will display any impcompatabilities, as well as allowing you to check out other people's completed builds. YouTube is another great site that allows you to look up things you are unsure about. There are full walkthroughs on YouTube that teaches you how to build a PC.
