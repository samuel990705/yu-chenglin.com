Future Upgrades

Upgrade Priority 1 - Second GPU

My first upgrade to my gaming PC would to get another graphics card to form a two-way SLI. With games that support two-way SLI, my PC can almost double the framerates of the game.

Upgrade Priority 2 - 4k Monitor

My second upgrade I would make to my gaming PC is to get another monitor. This not only allows me to have a dual monitor set up, but thats to my second GPU from the previous upgrade, my PC will definitely be able to run games at 4k resolution with a high fPS.
