Cryptocurrency GPU Inflation

Cryptocurrency

Due to the recent rise in popularity for crptocurrency mining, graphic card prices have skyrocketed to close to double the prices of their original market price. People are buying graphic cards, due to their high processing power, to mine cryptocurrencies. Therefore, the demand for GPUs have increased drastically, causing price to rise.

How Did I Avoid This

In order to avoid being affected by the inflation of GPU prices, you must subscribe to a email notification list that emails you whenever a GPU comes in stock. This will allow you to buy GPUs close to their market price. I used NowInStock to help me get a 1080 ti.
